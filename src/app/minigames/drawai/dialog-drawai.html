DALL·E Draw Challenge

Objective:

The objective of the DALL·E Draw Challenge is to test your artistic skills and creativity by drawing something related to a given theme on an HTML canvas. Your drawing will be compared to an image generated by the DALL·E AI model to see how well you captured the essence of the theme. The goal of this minigame is to show you, the player, to what extend AI can be creative and how it can be used to help you in your creative process.

Rules:

  1. Each round of the game consists of a specific theme provided to the player.
  2. You will have an unlimited amount of time to create your drawing on the HTML canvas.
  3. The drawing can be created using any drawing tools available on the canvas, such as brushes, pens, colors, and erasers.
  4. Be creative and interpret the theme in your unique way. Your drawing can be abstract or realistic, as long as it relates to the given theme.
  5. Avoid using any text or symbols in your drawing. The challenge focuses on visual expression through drawing.
  6. Once the drawing is finished, press on done and your drawing will compared to an image generated by the DALL·E AI model based on the same theme.
  7. The DALL·E-generated image will be presented alongside your drawing for comparison.
  8. The comparison will be based on the theme, composition, and overall similarity between your drawing and the DALL·E image.
  9. Players can engage in multiple rounds with different themes to improve their drawing skills and challenge themselves.

Tips:

  1. Pay attention to the theme and brainstorm different ideas before starting your drawing.
  2. Use various drawing techniques and experiment with colors and shapes to make your drawing visually appealing.
  3. Take advantage of the canvas tools, such as the colorpicker or free colour scale options, to refine your drawing.
  4. Don't worry about perfection. The focus is on expressing your creativity and interpretation of the theme.
  5. Analyze the DALL·E-generated image and your drawing side by side to understand the similarities and differences.
  6. Learn from each round and strive to enhance your skills and artistic expression.
  7. Have fun and enjoy the challenge of creating unique artwork!
